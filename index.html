FreeTaxi — Free rides for people on the front line

Volunteer-driven mobility service — app, drivers, and logistics to help civilians and responders in frontline areas.

We provide free, on-demand rides to civilians, medical teams, volunteers, and displaced people in active and recently liberated frontline regions of Ukraine. Our mission is to reduce barriers to safety and essential services during the conflict.

What we do

  • Mobile app to request a free ride (Android/iOS — links coming soon)
  • Volunteer driver network with verified IDs and background checks
  • Logistics coordination for medical transfers, supply drops, and evacuations

Problem we solve

Frontline civilians and volunteers often lack safe, reliable transport to reach medical care, evacuation points, shelters, or to move humanitarian cargo. Official transport channels are overloaded — we provide a community-driven alternative at no cost to users.

How it works

  1. User requests a ride in the app and selects a safe pickup zone.
  2. A verified volunteer driver accepts the request and follows secure routing protocols.
  3. Trip updates and safety confirmations are sent to an assigned coordinator.
